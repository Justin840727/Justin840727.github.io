About me

Hi, I'm Justin Zhang, a Research and Development engineer at Taipei Tech University, with experience developing autonomous robots in aspects of hardware , software system design. I'm fueled by my passion for solving any technical problems , enjoying the processes of solving a problem from surveying solutions by reading papers to implementing the solution on our system.

In the past five years, I have created three autonomous robots and performed those robots on four services, including campus delivery service, hospital delivery, e-scooter charging service, and shared-use autonomous bike. If you are interested to see those projects please check out the project section in my portfolio.

I personally like every outdoor activity, such as trekking, skateboarding, and fishing, doing those makes me feel relaxed and stress-less. More importantly, doing outdoor activities on weekends can increase my work efficiency. In the future, I would like to obtain degrees and work in the United States. Working in a country, that has established many Tech Giants, motivates me to create technologies that bring the world convenience and sustainability.
